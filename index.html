Bio

Experience

Since May 2016 I have been working at Deloitte UK as a Cyber Risk Consultant specialising in Identity and Access Management. My areas of focus include Digital Identity, Identity as a Service (IDaaS), cloud and DevOps.

Prior to that I was employed at American Express as an Incident Response Analyst. In the past I have worked as a junior Java developer both as an intern and under 2 month contracts. As an intern I was responsible for creating a Java library for efficient ontology handling and management. In addition, while working under short-term contracts I developed middleware components in Java.

Studies

In 2015 I completed my MSc in Information Security at University College London (UCL) with a GPA of 78.83% (Distinction) . The course included modules such as Computer and Network Security, Cryptography, Privacy Enhancing Technologies, Malware and Information Security Management. In the context of my dissertation entitled "The Malware Schism", I developed a system capable of classifying malware samples based on their network behaviour.

In 2014 I received my BSc in Telecommunications Science and Technology from the University of Peloponnese with a GPA of 8.83/10. The course included 50 modules such as Operating Systems, Java, Communication Networks 1&2, Distributed System Programming, Network Management and Software Engineering. My thesis, "Ontological Management of Security Incidents" dealt with the challenging task of mapping security incidents to an OWL ontology thus enabling efficient alert correlation.

In July 2013, I attended the Erasmus Intensive Programme on Pervasive Networks and Service Infrastructures “Perseus” which took place in Crete. Topics included Effective Network Utilisation, Ubiquitous Access, QoS/QoE, Multimedia, Efficient Energy Consumption and Network Security.

Interests

My interests include Digital Identity, Cloud, DevOps, Infrastructure as code, Computer and Network Security, Access Control, Security Policies, Threat Modeling, Malware Analysis, Traffic Analysis, Intrusion Detection, Applied Cryptography, Software Engineering, Semantic Web.
